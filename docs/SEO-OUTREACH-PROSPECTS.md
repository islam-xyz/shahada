# SEO outreach — link prospects for Shahada.org

Use this list for **ethical, manual outreach**: email or contact forms only. Do not buy links or use bulk spam.

## One-line pitch (adapt per audience)

> Shahada.org is a **free, open-source** site that helps people say the **Shahada** (declaration of faith) in **40+ languages**, with **audio**. We’d be grateful if you could add us under your “New Muslims”, “Resources”, or FAQ page: `https://shahada.org/en/` (or the visitor’s language, e.g. `https://shahada.org/nl/`).

**Suggested HTML snippet** (also on [Contribute](https://shahada.org/en/contribute.html)):

```html
<a href="https://shahada.org/">Shahada.org — free guide to the declaration of faith (40+ languages)</a>
```

## Suggested anchor text (natural)

- “how to say the Shahada” / “Shahada in many languages”
- “guide for new Muslims” (only if their page context matches)
- Brand: “Shahada.org”

## Email template (short)

**Subject:** Resource for new Muslims — Shahada.org (40+ languages)

**Body:**

Hello,

We maintain **Shahada.org**, a free, non-profit guide to saying the **Shahada** (declaration of faith), available in **40+ languages** with pronunciation audio.

If you maintain a resources page for **new Muslims** or visitors learning about Islam, would you consider adding a link?

- Dutch: https://shahada.org/nl/  
- English: https://shahada.org/en/  
- All languages: https://shahada.org/

The project is [open source on GitHub](https://github.com/islam-xyz/shahada) if you want to verify or suggest improvements.

Thank you for your time.

---

## What to avoid

- Paid link networks, PBNs, “SEO guest post” farms  
- Automated blog comments or forum signatures at scale  
- Misleading anchor text or hidden links  

These can trigger **manual actions** and hurt trust.

Ghusl: Te Horoi Tikanga o te Ihirama

Ko te Ghusl (Arapi: غُسْل) te horoi tinana katoa i roto i te Ihirama. I muri i te Shahada, ko te Ghusl te mahi tuatahi o te horoi ina tīmata te oranga hou hei Mahometa.

He aha te Ghusl?

Ko te Ghusl te tikanga Ihirama o te horoi i te tinana katoa ki te wai. He mahi wairua e whakapai ana i te tangata (tahir).

Ko te kupu "ghusl" nō te pūtake Arapi, ko tōna tikanga "horoi". He rerekē ki te wudu (horoi wāhanga), ka uru te tinana katoa ki te ghusl.

Āhea ka hiahiatia te Ghusl?

Ka hiahiatia (fard) te Ghusl i ēnei āhuatanga:

  • I muri i te whakaae ki te Ihirama — Tō ghusl tuatahi.
  • I muri i te ai — Ahakoa kāore he putanga.
  • I muri i te putanga — E moe ana, e oho ana rānei.
  • I muri i te mate wahine — Mō ngā wāhine.
  • I muri i te toto whānau — Mō ngā wāhine.

He tūtohu (sunnah) te ghusl i mua i te Rā Paraire, Eid, me te Ihram.

Me pēhea te mahi i te Ghusl

I runga i te hadith a Aisha (r.a.).

I kī a Aisha (r.a.) i a ia e ghusl ana mō te janaba, ka horoi ia i ōna ringa, ka horoi i tōna wāhi tūmataiti ki te ringa mauī, ka wudu pērā ki te wudu mō te inoi, ka tango wai me te whakauru i ngā matimati ki ngā pakiaka o ngā makawe ka riringi e toru kapuranga wai ki runga i te mātenga, kātahi ka riringi wai ki runga i te toenga o te tinana, ka horoi i ōna waewae.

— Sahih Muslim #479

Pātai Auau

Āhea ka hiahiatia?

Ai, putanga, mate wahine, nifas, tūhono ki te Ihirama.

Kāore he wudu?

Kei roto te wudu i te sunnah katoa.

He ahunga?

Kāo.

Makawe?

Wai ki ngā pakiaka.

Hei whakakapinga mō te wudu?

Āe.

I roto i te kāwei?

Āe.

He aha te rerekētanga?

Wudu he wāhanga; ghusl he katoa.

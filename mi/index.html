Hei Mahometa: Kōrerotia te Shahada

Ko te Shahada te whakapuakitanga whakapono Ihirama — te hikoi tuatahi ki te hei Mahometa. Kōrerotia mā te ngākau pono, ka hei Mahometa koe.

Te Whakamāoritanga

"Ka whakapono au kāore he atua engari ko Allah, ā, ka whakapono au ko Muhammad te karere a Allah."

He Aha te Shahada?

Ko te Shahada (Ārapia: الشَّهَادَة, "te whakaaturanga") te whakapuakitanga whakapono Ihirama, ā, ko te pou tuatahi o ngā Pou e Rima o te Ihirama. Koinei te kōrero nui rawa atu i roto i te Ihirama — te tatau e uru ai ngā Māhometa katoa ki te whakapono. Nō te pūtake Ārapia sh-h-d tēnei kupu, ko te tikanga "ki te whakaatu" rānei "ki te whakaū".

Ngā Wāhanga e Rua o te Shahada

  • "Kāore he atua engari ko Allah" (لَا إِلَٰهَ إِلَّا ٱللَّٰهُ) — Ka whakaū koe kotahi noa te Atua e tika ana ki te karakia. Ko tēnei te Tawhid (whakapono ki te Atua kotahi), te ngako o te Ihirama.
  • "Ko Muhammad te karere a Allah" (مُحَمَّدٌ رَسُولُ ٱللَّٰهِ) — Ka whakaae koe ko te Poropiti Muhammad (mau te rangimārie ki a ia) te karere whakamutunga a Allah, nāna i kawe mai te Quran.

Ko ēnei whakapuakitanga e rua te tūāpapa o te whakapono Ihirama. Mā te kōrero mā te whakapono pono, ka uru koe ki te Ihirama.

Kua Reri Koe?

Ina kua reri koe, kōrerotia te Shahada kia reo nui mā te whakapono pono. Kātahi ka pēhia te pātene kei raro nei.

Tō rā Shahada:

Kei tō taputapu anake tēnei rā e tiakina ana.

Ngā Mihi!

Ināianei he Mahometa koe. Nau mai ki te whakapono o te 1.8 piriona tāngata puta noa i te ao.

Mā Allah e manaaki tō haerenga. 🤲

Ko Te Aha Inaianei?

  1. Horoia ā-tikanga (Ghusl) — Ka horoi wairua tēnei i a koe ina tīmata tō oranga hou hei Mahometa.
  2. Akohia te karakia (Salah) — Tīmata ki ngā mea māmā. Ka karakia ngā Mahometa e rima ngā wā i te rā, engari akohia kia tōu tere ake.
  3. Honoa ki tētahi whare karakia — Kimihia tō hapori Mahometa ā-rohe mō te tautoko, te ārahi, me te hoa.

Ngā Pātai Auau

Me whai kaiwhakaatu au ki te hei Mahometa?

Kāo. Ko te Shahada he whakapuakitanga i waenganui i a koe me Allah. Ka pai ki ētahi te kōrero i roto i te whare karakia, i mua i ngā kaiwhakaatu rānei, engari ehara tēnā i te mea e hiahiatia ana.

Me kōrero au i te Shahada ki te reo Arapi?

Ahakoa he tikanga, he tūtohu hoki te reo Arapi, ko te mea nui ko te whakaaro pono me te mārama. Ki te kore e taea e koe te kōrero Arapi, ka whakaaetia te kōrero i tō reo ake mā te mārama katoa.

Ka taea e au te huri ki te Ihirama i te kāinga, ko au anake?

Āe, tino pai. Ko te Ihirama i waenganui i a koe me Allah. Kāore e hiahiatia he whare karakia, he imam, he tangata kē rānei. Kōrerotia te Shahada mā te whakapono pono, ka hei Mahometa koe.

Ka nui te kōrero i te Shahada ki te hei Mahometa?

Āe. Ko te Shahada te pou tuatahi, te pou nui rawa atu o te Ihirama. Mā te kōrero mā te whakapono, ka hei Mahometa koe. Ko ētahi atu mea katoa (karakia, nohopuku, me ētahi atu) ka taea te ako āta haere.

Kei konei tēnei whārangi hei āwhina i ngā tāngata ki te tango i tō rātou hikoi tuatahi ki te Ihirama.

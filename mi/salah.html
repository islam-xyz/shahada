Salah: Te Inoi Ihirama

Ko te Salat (Arapi: صَلَاة) te inoi tikanga Ihirama e mahia ana e rima ngā wā i ia rā. Ko te pou tuarua o te Ihirama.

He aha te Salah?

Ko te Salah te hononga tōtika i waenga i te Mahometa me Allah. E rima ngā wā i ia rā — Fajr, Dhuhr, Asr, Maghrib, Isha. Ia tahi he rak'ah.

I mua i te salah me parakore koe (ghusl, wudu rānei), ka anga ki te qiblah.

Rukn, Wājib, Sunnah

E toru ngā taumata:

  • Rukn (Arkān) — Kāore e taea te whakarere.
  • Wājib — Me mahi; sujūd sahwi.
  • Sunnah — He tūtohu.

  2. Takbīr

    "Allāhu Akbar".

  3. Al-Fātihah

  5. Te whakatika mai i te Rukū'

  7. Sujūd

  9. Te noho i waenga i ngā Sujūd

    Rak'ah whakamutunga.

    Tūnga tika.

  13. E rua ngā Salām

    "As-salāmu alaykum".

  14. Te raupapa tika

    I te raupapa tika.

Pātai

E rima ngā inoi?

Fajr(2), Dhuhr(4), Asr(4), Maghrib(3), Isha(4). Tapeke 17.

Mēnā he hapa?

Sujūd sahwi.

Arapi?

Fātihah me ngā adhkār.

Ahunga?

Qiblah — ki te Kaaba.

Rukn vs Wājib?

Kāore he rukn, kāore he inoi.

E noho ana?

Āe.

Kākahu?

Tāne: pito ki te turi. Wāhine: kanohi me ngā ringa anake.

Whariki?

Ehara i te mea me.
